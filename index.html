Stronger AI

by
Charles Iliya Krempeaux

a work in progress

Stronger AI is a book on creating human-like (or for a looser definition mammal-like) intelligence in silico. I.e., a book on creating strong AI. In addition to name strong AI, this topic also goes by the names: artificial general intelligence (AGI) and cognitive AI. Only a background in software engineering and an interest in strong AI are necessary to read this book.

The author created this book due to three motivations: #1 that the author had the inclination to write it, #2 that there seemed to be no books that introduced someone with only a software engineering background to the topic and #3 to help organize his views on topic.
